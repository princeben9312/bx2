About

In the heart of a quaint, idyllic town, the Savvanah Hospital has stood as a beacon of healing and hope for over a century. Established in 1890 by Dr. Eleanor Sinclair and Dr. Alexander Haviland, this revered institution quickly gained acclaim for its innovative medical practices. Over the years, Savvanah Hospital has been at the forefront of pioneering healthcare, from pioneering the use of "Savvanah's Serenity," a legendary herbal remedy, to introducing cutting-edge robotic surgery. Today, it continues to be a symbol of excellence in healthcare, blending tradition with innovation to serve generations of patients in their pursuit of well-being.
