Golden Retriever

    About

    These sturdy, medium-sized dogs grow to about 55 to 75 pounds and typically live to be about 10 to 12 years old. Outgoing, trustworthy, eager-to-please, and easy-to-train, they are especially popular with families with young children. Goldens often maintain their fun-loving puppy attitudes and signature smile into adulthood.
